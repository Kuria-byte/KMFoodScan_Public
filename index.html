Advertising & Planning

We know, advertising objectives are derived from marketing objectives. Therefore, the first step in our advertising planning is to review to the objectives as set out in the marketing plan

  • Increase Purchase

    Purchase frequency or time between orders is extremely useful for timing our outreach efforts

  • Encourage Trial

    A temporary offering intended to provide market information by allowing consumers to examine, use or test a product

  • Encourage Loyalty

    Implement a rewards program, incentivize customers to return on a regular basis

  • Position or re-position a brand

    positioning the place, a brand occupies in the mind of its target audience

  • Educate Customers

    By educating customers, we ensure that they understand the benefits and value of the product

  • Push & Pull Strategy

    Our push marketing takes the product to the consumer, whereas pull marketing brings the consumer to the product

  • Brand Awareness

    We know that brand awareness is a key consideration in consumer behavior, advertising management, brand management and strategy development

  • Brand Preference

    We provide the strength to a brand in the hearts and minds of customers, so, brands are preferred under assumptions of equality in price and availability
